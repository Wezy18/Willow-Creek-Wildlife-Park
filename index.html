About Us

Willow Creek Wildlife Park is a family-friendly, conservation-focused zoo nestled in the scenic woodlands of Oregon. Home to over 150 species from around the world, the park offers immersive habitats, hands-on educational programs, and guided wildlife experiences. Guests can explore themed regions such as the African Savanna, Pacific Northwest Trails, and Tropical Rainforest Dome. Dedicated to animal welfare and environmental education, Willow Creek is the perfect destination for nature lovers of all ages.
