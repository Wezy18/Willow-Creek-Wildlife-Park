Spring Hatch Day

Date: April 20

Time: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Location: Children's Discovery Barn

Details:

Celebrate spring and new life at Willow Creek! Guests can visit our hatchery to see newly hatched chicks and ducklings, enjoy egg-themed crafts, and join in our seasonal egg hunt (ages 2–10).
