Explore the Zoo

Welcome Area

Start your visit here! The Welcome Area includes ticket booths, an information center, the amphitheater for shows, and our gift shop full of fun souvenirs. Be sure to check the schedule for daily events!

Wildlife

This area is home to our most exotic creatures including tigers, reptiles, and animals in our nocturnal house. Walk through lush habitats and learn about conservation efforts around the world.

Farm Animals

Visit our friendly farmyard featuring goats, sheep, pigs, and chickens! This area also includes a petting zoo where kids can safely interact with animals up close and learn about life on the farm.
