Big Cat Awareness Weekend

Date: April 13–14

Time: 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Location: African Savanna Exhibit

Details:

Join us for a roaring weekend dedicated to lions, tigers, and leopards! Meet our zookeepers, enjoy live enrichment demos, and learn how big cats are protected in the wild. Kids can complete a special Big Cat Explorer badge activity.
